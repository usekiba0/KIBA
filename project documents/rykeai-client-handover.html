Day-to-Day Operations

Once live, the platform runs itself. Here's what normal operations look like and what you may occasionally need to do.

👁️
Monitoring users
View active users, subscriptions, and message activity in your Supabase dashboard at supabase.com/dashboard. Select your project → Table Editor → browse the users, subscriptions, and messages tables. No action needed — this is read-only monitoring.
💳
Monitoring payments
Your Stripe dashboard at dashboard.stripe.com shows all subscribers, trial statuses, revenue, and failed payments. Stripe automatically retries failed payments and sends the user an SMS. No manual action needed unless a user contacts you directly.
📱
Monitoring messages
Your Twilio Console at console.twilio.com → Monitor → Logs → Messages shows all inbound and outbound SMS. Useful to investigate if a user reports they didn't receive a message or their reply wasn't answered.
🚨
Responding to crisis alerts
When a crisis is detected you will receive an SMS and email alert. See Section 05 for the full step-by-step response process. This is the most time-sensitive operation — always respond within 5 minutes.
🗑️
User data deletion (GDPR)
If a user requests deletion of their data, contact your developer to run the GDPR deletion. This permanently removes all their messages, health data, and billing records. Their Stripe subscription is also cancelled automatically.
📤
User data export (GDPR)
If a user requests a copy of their data, contact your developer. They can export everything — messages, health info, nutrition logs, session summaries — as a downloadable file within minutes.
Section 05 — Critical

Handling Crisis Alerts

This is the most important section in this guide. When a user sends a message suggesting they may be in crisis, the platform automatically detects it and alerts you. Here is exactly what happens and what you must do.

🚨
Always respond to crisis alerts within 5 minutes

The user is waiting. The AI coaching is paused until you resolve the alert. Do not ignore these notifications or handle them later — a delayed response could have serious consequences.

What happens automatically

🔍
Step 01
Detected
Instant
AI detects crisis signal. Coaching reply suppressed.
⏸️
Step 02
Session Held
Instant
User's session is paused. AI will not reply until resolved.
💬
Step 03
Holding SMS Sent
Instant
User receives an empathetic message with 988 Crisis Line.
📲
Step 04
You Are Alerted
< 5 minutes
You receive SMS + email with the user's name and phone.

What you need to do

1
Read the alert SMS or email
It contains the user's name, phone number, and an Alert ID. Note the Alert ID — you will need it in step 4.
2
Call or text the user directly
Reach out to the user on their phone number. Check in personally. If they are in immediate danger, call 999 (UK), 911 (US), or your local emergency services.
3
Handle the situation as a human coach
Listen, support, and connect them with the right resources — their GP, a therapist, or emergency services if needed. You may also text them from the Ryke number manually during this time.
4
Resolve the alert to restore AI coaching
Once the user is safe and you are satisfied, contact your developer with the Alert ID to mark it resolved. This restores normal AI coaching and sends the user a gentle follow-up message: "Your coach has checked in. I'm here whenever you're ready — no rush. 💙"
💡
What the user receives automatically

The user gets one of these holding messages before you even see the alert: "Thank you for trusting me with this. What you're feeling is real and it matters. I'm connecting you with someone who can support you — please hang tight. If this is urgent, text 988 (Crisis Line) now."

Section 06

Stripe & Billing

All billing is managed automatically by Stripe. You do not need to manually charge or cancel any user. Here is how the billing lifecycle works.

1
User signs up → Free trial starts
Card is saved securely by Stripe. No charge is made. Trial lasts 30 days. User receives a welcome SMS.
2
3 days before trial ends → Reminder SMS sent
User automatically receives a text reminding them the trial ends in 3 days. They can reply CANCEL to stop.
3
Trial ends → $20/month charge begins
Stripe charges the saved card. Coaching continues uninterrupted. User receives a confirmation SMS.
4
Payment fails → Automatic retry + SMS notice
If a charge fails, Stripe retries and sends the user an SMS asking them to update their payment method. Account is set to "past due" but coaching continues briefly.
5
Cancellation → Account deactivated
User texts CANCEL or cancels from their Stripe customer portal. They receive a farewell SMS. Their account is deactivated and no further charges occur.
💰
Your revenue dashboard

Log into dashboard.stripe.com to see your monthly recurring revenue, active subscribers, trial conversions, and churn rate. All financial reporting is live and real-time in your Stripe account.

⚠️

Section 08

Common Issues

Most issues have simple explanations. Here are the most common ones and what to do.

A user texted Ryke but got no reply
1. Check if the user is on crisis hold — log into Supabase and search their phone number in the users table. If crisis_hold = true, they need a coach resolution before AI replies resume.
2. Check Twilio message logs for delivery errors.
3. Confirm the user's phone number was submitted with a country code (e.g. +1 for US).
4. If none of the above, contact your developer with the user's phone number.
The onboarding form is showing an error after payment
The most common cause is a phone number format issue (missing country code like +1) or the Stripe API is in test mode but the user used a real card. Confirm BETA_MODE is turned off for live users, and that Stripe live keys are in place. Contact your developer with the error message shown.
I'm not receiving crisis alert SMS/emails
Check that CRISIS_COACH_ALERT_PHONE and CRISIS_COACH_ALERT_EMAIL in your server settings match your current phone number and email. Also confirm SMTP email settings are configured if you expect email alerts. Contact your developer to verify.
A user says they were charged but their account shows cancelled
Log into Stripe Dashboard → Customers and search by the user's email or phone. You can see the full payment history. If there is a billing discrepancy, use Stripe's built-in Refund button to issue a refund directly. This does not require developer involvement.
Ryke replied to a user in a way that seems wrong or off-brand
The AI coaching replies are generated by Claude (Anthropic's AI). For serious concerns — factually incorrect advice, inappropriate responses, or safety-related issues — contact your developer with the specific message. The system prompt can be tuned to adjust Ryke's tone and guardrails.
The health check URL shows "degraded"
This means either the database (Supabase) or the session cache (Upstash Redis) is not responding. Check your Supabase and Upstash dashboards for outages. If both services show as healthy on their dashboards, contact your developer.
Section 09

Frequently Asked Questions

Can I change the $20/month price?
Yes — go to Stripe Dashboard → Product Catalogue → RYKE AI Individual → add a new price. Then ask your developer to update the STRIPE_PRICE_ID_INDIVIDUAL setting to the new price ID. Existing subscribers keep their current price; new sign-ups get the new price.
Can I change the free trial length from 30 days?
Yes. Ask your developer to update the STRIPE_TRIAL_DAYS setting. Common options are 7, 14, or 30 days. This applies to new sign-ups only.
Can I add more coaching personas (e.g. a nutrition specialist voice)?
Yes — this requires a small development change to the AI system prompt. The Coach Pro and Coach Elite plans support multiple AI personas. Contact your developer to discuss customisation.
How do I give a user a free trial extension?
Log into Stripe Dashboard → find the customer → find their subscription → click "Update subscription" → change the trial end date. No developer action required.
Can I see what users are saying to Ryke?
All messages are stored in your Supabase database in the messages table. You can read them there. For privacy best practice, only review messages when necessary (e.g. a reported complaint or a crisis situation).
What happens if Anthropic's AI goes down?
If the Claude API is temporarily unavailable, the AI coaching replies will pause. The crisis detection system treats any API failure as a potential crisis (it defaults to safe — if unsure, it assumes danger). Anthropic's uptime is 99.9%+ — short outages are rare and self-resolve.
Is user health data stored securely?
Yes. All data is stored in your private Supabase database with SSL encryption. Conversations are not shared with third parties or used to train any AI. You are the data controller. Supabase is GDPR-compliant and SOC 2 Type 2 certified.
How do I add a Coach Pro plan for professional coaches?
Create a new product in Stripe at $99/month and share the price ID with your developer. The Coach Pro features (branded SMS number, coach dashboard, multi-client management) require additional development — contact your developer to scope and schedule this work.
