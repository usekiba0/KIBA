Who We Serve

Three Personas, One Platform

RYKE AI serves anyone with a phone and a goal. These three archetypes represent our core user base during Phase 1.

A
Alex
Fitness Beginner

25 years old, works a desk job. Wants to build a consistent workout habit but doesn't know where to start and can't afford a personal trainer.

  • Get a simple weekly workout plan via SMS
  • Receive daily encouragement to stay on track
  • Learn proper form through text-based guidance
  • Track progress without a complicated app
M
Maya
Nutrition Seeker

32 years old, busy parent. Wants to eat better and lose 10 lbs but struggles with meal planning and mindless snacking between meetings.

  • Get daily meal ideas based on her preferences
  • Understand macros without complex tracking apps
  • Receive accountability check-ins each morning
  • Ask quick food questions and get instant answers
J
Jordan
Wellness User

40 years old, high-stress career. Experiences anxiety and poor sleep. Wants a daily mental wellness touchpoint without therapy-level commitment.

  • Start each day with a grounding or mindfulness prompt
  • Vent without judgment and get calm, practical responses
  • Build a breathing or wind-down habit via reminders
  • Feel heard — not processed — by an AI coach

Phase Seven

Long-term Retention

For users who go quiet, RYKE AI re-engages with empathy — not spam. For active users, the coaching evolves with them as their goals deepen and change.

  • Re-engagement message sent if no reply in 3+ days
  • Tone stays warm — no guilt-tripping or pushy nudges
  • Active users see goals evolve: beginner → intermediate
  • Users can pause, switch focus (fitness → nutrition), or reset

Safety Net

When It Matters Most

RYKE AI is built with a non-negotiable safety layer. If a user shows signs of distress, the system acts immediately — no exceptions, no configuration can disable this.

🛡️

Crisis Detection & Human Handoff Protocol

Triggered by distress keywords, repeated negative sentiment, or crisis language — automatically and silently.

⚠️
Signal Detected
Distress keyword or crisis language identified in user's message
💬
Holding Message
Supportive SMS sent to user immediately while coach is alerted
📣
Coach Alerted
Coach notified via SMS/email within 5 minutes SLA
🤝
Human Takes Over
AI steps back; human coach handles the conversation directly
Example Holding Message (sent automatically to user)
"Thank you for trusting me with this. What you're feeling is real and it matters. I'm connecting you with someone who can support you properly — please hang tight. If this is an emergency, text or call 988 (Suicide & Crisis Lifeline)."
Non-Negotiable — Cannot be disabled Safety Gate ✓ 5-minute SLA Phase 1: Email/SMS alert · Phase 2: Full dashboard
